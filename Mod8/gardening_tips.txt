Tip 1: Be aware of sun exposure of your chosen spot over the course of a day, and choose the correct plant accordingly!
Tip 2: Make sure to use mulch to protect the plant's roots, this will increase it's chances of success!
Tip 3: It is best to water first thing in the morning, and early. This helps moisture be absorbed before the sun evaporates it, protects the leaves from being burned in the process, and protects the plant from fungal growth which occurs when mulch being damp overnight.
